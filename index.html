What I'm building

Entuned

Original, adaptive soundtracks tuned to each store's customers, and re-tuned continuously from dwell time, basket size and conversion. Multi-zone stores get different audio per zone. The music meets the shopper where they already are and gives them room to do what they came in to do. Built on the research.
