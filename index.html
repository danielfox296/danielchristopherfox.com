The premise

Music gets called soul-driven, magical, irreducibly human. I've spent thousands of hours inside it, and I don't see magic. I see a language. Patterned, learnable, shared. That's not a smaller view of music. It's what makes music move anyone at all.

And it's why the room can be tuned on purpose. If sound is craft rather than mysticism, then its effect on behavior isn't luck. It's design. That's the work.
