What I'm building

Entuned

Original, adaptive soundtracks tuned to each store's customers, and re-tuned continuously from dwell time, basket size and conversion. Multi-zone stores get different audio per zone. Every pilot runs against a matched control. The music meets the shopper where they already are and gives them room to do what they came in to do.
